1.2 On-Device Data (Steve — AI Mentor)

Steve, our on-device AI mentor, runs entirely on your iPhone using the Apple Neural Engine. All conversations are processed locally and are never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

On devices without A17 Pro or newer, Steve uses a privacy-preserving server model with:

  • Zero log retention — data discarded immediately after response generation
  • Full data isolation per request
  • No model training on user data

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services. We may collect minimal, anonymized crash reports via Apple's built-in crash reporting.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
