Kesef Studio Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 10, 2026

Kesef Studio is built on the principle of Sovereign Engineering. We prioritize your privacy and local-first autonomy.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in via Apple or Google, we collect your email and name to establish your identity.
  • Profile & Activity: We store your matchmaking preferences, startup profile data, and workspace activity (Kanban boards, chats, uploaded files) securely via our backend infrastructure (Supabase) to sync across your devices.

2. The "Steve" AI (Local Processing)

Our on-device AI assistant, "Steve", operates entirely locally on your Apple hardware. No AI chat data, prompts, or proprietary startup ideas discussed with Steve are sent to our servers or third-party cloud APIs. Your conversations with the AI remain strictly on your device.

3. Account Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can completely and permanently delete your account, matches, and data directly within the Kesef Studio app via Profile > Settings > Delete Account.

4. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact: support@kesefplus.app
