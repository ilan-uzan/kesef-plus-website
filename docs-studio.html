Getting Started Steve AI Workspace Matchmaking FAQ

Getting Started

Download Kesef Studio from TestFlight. Create your account using Apple Sign In for the fastest onboarding experience.

System Requirements

iPhone running iOS 17 or later. For on-device Steve AI, iPhone 15 Pro or later (A17 Pro chip) is recommended.

Onboarding Flow

After sign-in, you'll select your role (technical, creative, business), your startup stage, and your intent (build, explore, join). This powers your matchmaking and AI experience.

Steve AI Guide

Steve is your on-device AI startup mentor. He runs entirely on the Neural Engine — no cloud, full privacy.

What Steve Can Do

Challenge bad ideas, restructure roadmaps, generate task checklists, analyze your workspace context, and provide structured mentorship based on proven startup frameworks.

Privacy

All Steve conversations are processed locally. We never see, store, or train on your conversations.

Workspace Features

Your startup workspace includes Kanban boards, milestone tracking, file sharing, and team management — all connected to Steve for intelligent automation.

Matchmaking

Swipe to match with co-founders, designers, and developers. Matches are based on skills, stage, location preferences, and intent compatibility.

FAQ

Is Kesef Studio free?

During beta, all features are free. We'll announce pricing before the public launch.

Can I use Steve without an internet connection?

Yes — on supported devices (A17 Pro+), Steve runs entirely offline.
