Getting Started

Download Kesef Demo from TestFlight. No account required — just import screenshots and start creating.

Creating Videos

Import 3–10 screenshots, arrange them in order, choose a template and soundtrack, then export. The AI handles transitions, panning, and timing automatically.

Device Framing

Automatically detect device type from screenshot dimensions. Choose from every current Apple device frame in every color option.

Export Formats

Export in App Store preview dimensions (iPhone, iPad), social media formats (Instagram, X, TikTok), Product Hunt gallery, or 16:9 widescreen for pitch decks.
